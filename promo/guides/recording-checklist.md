# Recording Checklist

Run through this before every recording session.

## Before You Start

- [ ] Close all unnecessary apps (Slack, email, Discord) — reduces CPU load and prevents notification popups
- [ ] Set Windows to Do Not Disturb — press `Win + A`, enable Focus Assist > Alarms Only
- [ ] Open only Chrome/Edge with TradeMetrics Pro logged in at trademetricspro.com
- [ ] Hide browser bookmarks bar — press `Ctrl+Shift+B`
- [ ] Zoom browser to 110% — press `Ctrl++` for better readability on recordings
- [ ] Clear browser tabs — only trademetricspro.com should be open (close all other tabs)
- [ ] For Series 1 (Copy My Trades) demos: make sure both MT5 terminals are open with Master and Follower EAs running
- [ ] Navigate to the starting page for your script before recording

## OBS Checks

- [ ] Correct OBS profile loaded — check top menu bar shows the right profile name (YouTube / TikTok / Twitter)
- [ ] Preview shows the correct scene — verify no personal information is visible (email, other tabs, desktop icons)
- [ ] Audio meter responding — speak normally and confirm green bars are bouncing in the Audio Mixer panel at the bottom
- [ ] Test record 5 seconds — hit Record, wait 5s, hit Stop, play the file back to verify both video and audio quality
- [ ] Disk space check — at least 5 GB free (a 10-minute MKV recording at 8Mbps is roughly 600MB)

## Your Setup

- [ ] Glass of water nearby — dry mouth kills voiceover quality, especially after several takes
- [ ] Script open on second monitor or phone — do NOT tab away from the recording to read the script
- [ ] Quiet room — close windows, turn off fans/AC if possible, wait for noisy appliances to stop
- [ ] Phone on silent (not just vibrate — vibrations can be picked up by laptop mic)

## Ready

Hit Record. Follow the script section by section. You can always pause and re-record a section — edit out the pause in post.

**Tip:** If you mess up a voiceover line, just pause for 2 seconds (makes it easy to find in editing), then re-say the line from the beginning. Cut the mistake in post.
